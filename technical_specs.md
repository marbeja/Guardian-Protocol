    DE: Nutzt BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) Beacons, um die Distanz zu Einsatzkräften zu triasulieren, wenn GPS < 3m Genauigkeit fällt.

    ES: Utiliza balizas BLE para triangular la distancia con los servicios de emergencia cuando el GPS pierde precisión.

    EN: Uses BLE beacons to triangulate distance for emergency responders when GPS accuracy drops below 3m.

B. Forensic Audio Ringbuffer (Cloud-Sync)

    DE: 300-Sekunden-Puffer. Bei Trigger (Keyword) wird der Puffer verschlüsselt und via IPFS/Cloud gesichert.

    ES: Búfer de 300 segundos. Al activarse (palabra clave), el audio se cifra y se sube a la nube/IPFS.

    EN: 300-second buffer. Upon trigger, audio is encrypted and synced to cloud/IPFS for forensic evidence.
